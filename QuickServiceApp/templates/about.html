For entrepreneurs:

We all are affected in the lockdown where people prefer online shopping rather than coming to shop to purchase. It has been observed that now a days lockdown or no, people prefer online shopping rather than going to stores. This has affected a lot of people and many had to even shut down their shops due to losses. This has made all small-scale entrepreneurs to think a way out of this situation and increase the sale. Many of you think how to bring your shop online but, all of you have the same question that is how?

Do not worry we have a common solution for consumers as well as entrepreneurs. Our website connects consumers to the local stores to buy and sell stuff online with home delivery option.

How it Works

Shops:

Local shops can register on our website and add the information of their products. When a consumer places order it will be visible as soon as you log into your account along with the consumer address, name and contact number. For payment we have cash on delivery. When a consumer receives the order that order will disappear from the website.

Consumers:

When you register into our website the pin-code that you enter will be used to show you the shops in your locality distributed category wise for your convenience. When you will view a particular store, you will be able to see all the available products listed category wise and the map of that particular store will be available if provided by the shop. You can click on the product you want to purchase and the details of that product will be available along with a add to cart option. After placing order, you can view that in orders option where on receiving a delivery you need to click on received button to inform that you have received your product always remember to do this step as doing this will verify the shop that you have received the order. In the navigation bar you can access your cart, previous orders placed, orders which are yet to be delivered and a sign out option.
